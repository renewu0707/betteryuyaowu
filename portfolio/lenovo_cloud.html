INTRODUCTION

COSN was short for Cloud OS Network, which was a IaaS public cloud service portal built on top of Microsoft Windows Azure Pack, to allow our customer offering VMs(Virtual Machine) hosting and leasing service to their tenants as a service provider. The portal had three sub-portals when sign-in as different roles - Cloud Computing Portal for Tenant Admin to apply for VMs, modify service plans, etc, Self-service Portal for Tenant Admin & Tenant Users to manage their VMs, and Cloud Service Management Portal for Service Provider.
