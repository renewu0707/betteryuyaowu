INTRODUCTION

"How Well I Draw" is a self-graded drawing panel that I designed for my multimedia class during the graduate study in HKUST(The Hong Kong University of Science & Technology, together with 2 team members. The goal is to design a user-friendly painting panel which users can not only practice their drawings on and save their drawings but also get a reasonable mark. We explored 3 existing algorithms: Histogram Matching, Matrix Decomposition adn Feature point based, and then designed our own similarity algorithm. Since the drawing panel aimed for kids, the whole design was lovely and colorful to attract kids' attention, and together with the joyful music, it would bring a lot of fun.

The drawing panel was developed based on Java.(jre 1.6, jdk 1.6)
